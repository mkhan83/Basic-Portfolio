About Me

FAC-C Level III Certified Senior Contracting Officer (unlimited CO Warrant Level) with over 10 years of experience at the General Services Administration having served in the Integrated Technology Services Strategic Solutions Division (ITS), Emergency Acquisitions Branch (EAB), and the Financial & Business Solutions Schedule (FABS). Highly experienced in working in a team environment to solve complex problems by identifying and engaging stakeholders, understanding program strengths and weaknesses, developing acquisition strategies, and performing both pre and post award functions. Served in various offices within GSA as a procurement professional and team leader with experience in Category Management, Strategic Sourcing, Vendor Management and Contract Negotiation. Responsibilities include engaging, developing, and securing key relationships with external industry partners (agencies such as Department of Homeland Security, DoD, HUD and Veterans Affairs). Experienced acquisition professional with proven success in development and management of highly complex, politically sensitive multi million dollar acquisitions. Experienced in awarding and administering multiple award schedule contracts, open market contracts, blanket purchase agreements (BPAs), and fixed price IDIQ time and material (T&M) task orders against MAS contracts.
